Pideli Kofte

Ingredients

  • 500 g ground beef
  • 1 onion, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 pita breads
  • 2 cups yogurt
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the ground beef, grated onion, minced garlic,
  2. breadcrumbs, egg, cumin, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  3. Form the mixture into small patties.
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat and cook the patties until browned on both sides.
  5. Warm the pita breads and serve the patties with yogurt and fresh parsley.
