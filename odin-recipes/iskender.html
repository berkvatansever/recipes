Iskender Kebab

Ingredients

  • 1 kg lamb, cut into thin slices
  • 500 g pita bread, cut into small pieces
  • 200 g yogurt
  • 100 g butter
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat a pan and cook the lamb slices until browned.
  2. In a separate pan, melt the butter and add the pureed tomatoes.
  3. Mix the cooked lamb with the tomato sauce.
  4. Place the pita bread pieces on a serving plate.
